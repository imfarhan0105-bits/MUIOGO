# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive collaboration
environment for all participants.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Provide actionable, technically grounded feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on project outcomes.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or hostile conduct.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after feedback.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through repository maintainers via private channels.
Maintainers will review reports and take appropriate action.
